Craft Your Summer Worldschooling Adventure

Step 1

Choose Your Theme

Select an action-based theme:

  • Take on a challenge (e.g., cycle around Taiwan)
  • Embark on a mission (e.g., collect 10 local stories)
  • Practice a lifestyle change (e.g., minimalist travel)
Step 2

Plan Your Journey

  • Set a duration (1-3 weeks)
  • Include kids in theme selection
  • Research background information
Step 3

Daily Documentation

  • Draw pictures
  • Record voice notes
  • Write in a travel diary
Step 4

Reflect & Share

  • Document the journey
  • Hold a family recap session
  • Share your story with others
